1. Introduction and Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how SkyFlix ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. SkyFlix is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Important: This privacy policy applies to the SkyFlix iOS application and any related services. By using SkyFlix, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

🚫 Zero Data Collection Policy: SkyFlix operates on a strict "no data collection" principle.

2.1 Personal Information

None Collected: SkyFlix does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Device identifiers or unique device IDs
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Payment information (handled by Apple)

2.2 Technical Information

Minimal Technical Data: The only technical information processed is:

  • Local app preferences and settings
  • Study progress and watch history (stored locally)
  • Favorites and bookmarks (stored locally)

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

3. How We Use Information

No Data Processing: Since we don't collect any information, there is no data processing, analysis, or external transmission.

3.1 Local Data Usage

The minimal local data is used solely for:

  • Providing personalized learning experience
  • Tracking study progress and completion
  • Managing user preferences and settings
  • Enabling offline functionality

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Location

  • Local Storage Only: All app data is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't use cloud services to store your information
  • No External Servers: Your data never leaves your device
  • No Database Storage: We don't maintain any external databases

4.2 Security Measures

Data security is maintained through:

  • Your device's built-in security features
  • iOS app sandboxing and data protection
  • Local encryption for sensitive app data
  • No network transmission of personal information

5. Third-Party Services and Integrations

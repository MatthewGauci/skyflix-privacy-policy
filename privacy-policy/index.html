Privacy Policy for SkyFlix

Last updated: August 16, 2024

Summary: SkyFlix is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

No Personal Information: SkyFlix does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you.

2. How We Use Information

No Data Processing: Since we don't collect any information, there is no data processing or analysis.

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage Only: All app data is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't use cloud services to store your information
  • No External Servers: Your data never leaves your device

4. Third-Party Services

  • No Analytics: We don't use analytics services
  • No Advertising: We don't display third-party advertisements
  • No Tracking: We don't use tracking technologies

5. In-App Purchases

  • Apple's System: All purchases are processed through Apple's secure system
  • No Payment Data: We never see or store your payment information
  • Purchase History: Stored locally on your device only

6. Content Access

  • Local Content: All educational content is bundled with the app
  • No Downloads: No content is downloaded from external sources
  • Offline Functionality: App works completely offline

7. Children's Privacy

SkyFlix is designed for aviation students and professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, security is maintained through your device's built-in security features.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact Information

Questions about this Privacy Policy?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@skyflix.app

11. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. All your app data is stored locally and can be managed through your device settings.

SkyFlix is committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure learning environment.
